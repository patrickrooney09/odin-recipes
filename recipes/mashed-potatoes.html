Mashed Potatoes

Description

Introducing the greatest mashed potatoes of all time. Pro tip: ricer. Butter, butter, more butter. Thats it.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes- (Russet/Idaho)
  • 1 cup Heavy Whipping cream or Half and Half
  • 1 stick of butter per 6 potatoes
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Peel, halve, and boil potatoes until "Fork tender"(you can put a fork through them with no resistance)- but careful not to overboil- as soon as they are fork tender, they are done.
  2. Meanwhile heat cream and butter together. You may have to eyeball the amount of cream and add more once you add this mixture to potatoes
  3. Drain Potatoes, rice or mash, add heated cream and butter. Salt and pepper to taste!
