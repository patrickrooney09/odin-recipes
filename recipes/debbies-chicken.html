Debbies Chicken

Perhaps the best "breaded" chicken out there. So crispy. Yes.

Ingredients

  • Skinless, boneless chicken breasts pounded thin.
  • Panko bread crumbs- seasoned or plain
  • Flour
  • One egg
  • Milk
  • Paprika to season
  • Artichokes (quartered)
  • Capers
  • Sundried Tomatoes- chopped

Instructions

  1. Create a shallow bowl of flour- then another one with egg/ milk whisked together, and another with the breadcrumb mixture
  2. Take pounded- flattened chicken breast and dredge through flour, egg, and panko crumbs. Set aside to rest
  3. When ready- pan fry in olive oil and buter mixture until golden.(keep in a warm oven ~180 degrees if cooking in a large quantity).
  4. Warm up a mixture (in same pan with fried bits) of capers, artichokes, sundried tomatoes. Toss over chicken and serve.
